What Metabolomics Measures

Eight biological domains in one test

The core distinction

Standard blood tests measure whether vitamins and minerals are present in the blood. Metabolomics measures whether your biological pathways are actually functioning — the downstream metabolic consequences of enzymatic activity. These are fundamentally different questions with different clinical implications.

Mitochondrial function — Krebs cycle intermediates, carnitine, CoQ10
Neurotransmitter metabolism — serotonin, dopamine, noradrenaline markers
B-vitamin functional status — methylmalonate (B12), xanthurenic acid (B6), FIGLU (folate)
Oxidative stress & detoxification — 8-OHdG, glutathione, phase II capacity
Amino acid metabolism — 40 amino acids, protein bottlenecks, nitrogen balance
Gut health markers — indican, D-arabinitol, dysbiosis indicators in urine

When to Use Metabolomics

The test that answers why.

Primary Indication

Chronic Fatigue & ME/CFS

Krebs cycle organic acids reveal exactly where in energy production the blockage lies — succinate accumulation, carnitine insufficiency, CoQ10 depletion. The mechanistic level that standard blood panels cannot reach.

Primary Indication

Treatment-Resistant Depression

Neurotransmitter metabolites (HIAA for serotonin, HVA and VMA for dopamine) provide objective measurement of neurotransmitter system function — explaining why SSRIs may not work and what the biochemical bottleneck is.

Primary Indication

Complex Multi-System Disease

Long COVID, fibromyalgia, MCAS and other complex presentations where the mechanism is unknown. The metabolomics pattern frequently reveals the biological system under greatest stress — guiding the GCR hierarchy.

NutrEval + Alzheimer's Assessment

For cognitive investigation, NutrEval is combined with p-Tau217 (Alzheimer's Assessment #5400). NutrEval reveals the modifiable mechanisms — mitochondrial dysfunction, homocysteine, neurotransmitter metabolism. p-Tau217 measures amyloid risk. Together they provide risk stratification plus a personalised intervention plan.
