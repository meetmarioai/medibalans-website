Diagnostic Tools

Tests that go deeper

Standard blood panels operate at serum level. Our diagnostics work at cellular, genetic and immunological level — where conditions actually originate.

01 · Immunology
ALCAT — Food Intolerance Analysis
The world's most validated test for delayed immune reactions to 200+ foods, additives, moulds and chemicals. Identifies hidden immune drivers behind IBS, fatigue, skin conditions and autoimmunity that standard allergy tests miss entirely.
Cell Science Systems · Validated
02 · Cellular Nutrition
CMA — Cellular Micronutrient Analysis
Measures 42 micronutrients directly inside white blood cells. The only clinically relevant way to assess true cellular nutrient status — serum levels may appear normal while cells are profoundly deficient.
Spectracell · Intracellular
03 · Epigenetics
MethylDetox — Methylation Profile
The market's only methylation test analysing 38 genes and all SNPs. Maps your complete methylation capacity — governing energy production, detoxification, hormonal balance, mood regulation and neurological function.
38 Genes · All SNPs
04 · Autonomic
HRV — Heart Rate Variability
Objective measurement of autonomic nervous system function and biological stress response. Assesses mitochondrial efficiency, recovery capacity and physiological resilience — a key marker in chronic fatigue and burnout.
Autonomic · Mitochondria
05 · Microbiome
GI Effects® Stool Profile
Genova Diagnostics comprehensive microbiome profiling — microbial diversity, pathogen identification, intestinal barrier integrity markers (zonulin, secretory IgA, calprotectin) and digestive function.
Genova Diagnostics
06 · Hormones
DUTCH Complete Hormone Panel
Comprehensive dried urine test for sex hormones, adrenal hormones and cortisol pattern. Measures hormone metabolites to reveal how hormones are processed — not just produced.
Genova · Dried Urine
07 · Longevity
MediBalans BioAge™
Patented biological age measurement via RNA transcriptome analysis (42,000 biomarkers), proteomic profiling and translational gap analysis. Measures how your body is actually ageing at cellular level.
Dante Labs · Patented
08 · NLS
NLS Body Scan
Non-invasive full-body bioresonance analysis mapping electromagnetic signals across 100+ organs and tissues. Used as clinical orientation to direct deeper targeted testing — never as a standalone diagnosis.
Non-invasive · Orientation
09 · Genomics
Whole Genome Sequencing
Complete 30x whole genome sequencing via Dante Labs. The most comprehensive genetic map available — identifying hereditary risk patterns, pharmacogenomic profiles and personalised longevity factors.
Dante Labs · WGS 30x

The Process

From booking to treatment

01
Initial Consultation
90-minute clinical deep-dive. We review your complete history, symptom patterns, previous investigations and genetic context. You leave with a clear diagnostic plan.
Day 1 · 90 min
02
Testing & Sample Collection
Blood draw, stool sample, urine collection — depending on your selected package. NLS body scan conducted at the clinic. Samples dispatched to specialist laboratories.
Day 1–3
03
Laboratory Analysis
Results processed at Spectracell, Cell Science Systems, Genova Diagnostics or Dante Labs depending on test type. Standard turnaround 2–4 weeks.
Weeks 2–4
04
Clinical Interpretation
Results interpreted by Dr Mario Anthis against your full clinical picture using the Global Constraint Rule framework. The primary biological constraint is identified and ranked.
Week 4–5
05
Protocol Presentation
60-minute results consultation. We walk through every finding, explain the biological mechanisms and present your personalised treatment protocol in full.
Week 5 · 60 min
06
Treatment & Follow-Up
Protocol initiated. IV therapy if indicated. Follow-up measurements scheduled at defined intervals. Protocol adjusted based on objective biological response data.
