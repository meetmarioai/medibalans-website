Why Genova Diagnostics

Data you cannot get elsewhere

Genova is not a standard laboratory. Their tests are specifically designed to answer clinical questions that conventional diagnostics leave unanswered.

Since 1986 — validated protocols

Genova Diagnostics was founded in 1986 and has decades of clinical validation behind its panels. Their methodology is published in peer-reviewed research and used by functional medicine clinics worldwide.

Functional, not just reference

Genova tests measure the metabolic consequences of enzymatic function — whether biological pathways have sufficient cofactors to operate. A patient can have normal serum B12 while methylmalonic acid (B12's functional marker) is elevated, indicating the vitamin is not being converted to its active form at cell level. This functional deficiency is completely missed by standard serum testing.

Clinical interpretation always included

Via MediBalans you never receive a test result without context. Every Genova report is clinically interpreted and translated into concrete actions — not just reference intervals on paper.
