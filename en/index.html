The Clinic

Medicine that begins with measurement

MediBalans is a clinic for precision and regenerative medicine in Stockholm. We receive patients with complex, chronic conditions that conventional healthcare has been unable to resolve — conditions requiring deeper investigation and more individualised treatment.

Our method is built on a simple principle: measure first, treat second. We use advanced diagnostics — intracellular nutritional analysis, immune reactivity testing, methylation panels and genetic mapping — to identify the molecular root causes driving a condition.

Treatment protocols are then individually tailored, based on the specific biological profile. Follow-up is conducted through objective measurements, not subjective assessments. We adjust based on what the data shows — not what should theoretically work.

With a patent portfolio in precision medicine, biomonitoring and regenerative therapies, we also conduct research and development of next-generation medical tools.

The Method

From measurement to regeneration

Every patient follows a structured clinical process. We measure, analyse, treat and verify — systematically and with full transparency.

01

Clinical Consultation

A thorough review of your medical history, symptom profile, previous treatments and biological goals. We identify which systems need mapping and which diagnostic tools will provide answers.

02

Precision Measurement

Targeted analyses based on the clinical picture: ALCAT panels for immune reactivity, cellular nutrient analysis (CMA), methylation panels, genetic mapping and gut barrier markers. We measure what is needed — nothing more, nothing less.

03

Pattern Analysis

Results are interpreted not in isolation but as an interconnected biological system. This is where patterns emerge that conventional tests miss — intracellular deficiencies, silent immune reactions, methylation blockades and the connections between them.

04

Individualised Protocol

Your treatment plan is built from your biological profile. Targeted supplementation, tailored as needed. Immune-modulating dietary adjustments based on your specific reactivities. Regenerative interventions where clinically indicated.

05

Verification & Regeneration

We retest at planned intervals to measure objective biological change. Protocols are adjusted based on measured data. The goal is not symptom relief but verified correction of the underlying imbalances — the prerequisite for regeneration.

Targeted Treatment

IV & IM Vitamin Therapy

When diagnostics reveal intracellular deficiencies, oral supplementation is not always sufficient. Gut inflammation, impaired absorption and genetic variants in transport proteins can limit how much reaches the cells via digestion.

IV and IM therapy delivers nutrients directly to the bloodstream or muscle tissue — at therapeutic concentrations that oral supplementation cannot achieve. Every treatment is selected based on your diagnostic profile, not as a general health boost.

We use exclusively Hydroxocobalamin as our B12 form — the natural form, free from cyanide, with a longer biological half-life and superior ability to support the body's methylation processes.

All treatments are preceded by an individual assessment. IV and IM therapy is prescribed based on clinical indication and diagnostic findings — not on request.

Why IV/IM?

Absorption
Close to 100% bioavailability vs 10–30% orally
Indication
Verified intracellular deficiency, impaired gut absorption, acute correction
Duration
IV infusion: 30–60 min. IM injection: a few minutes
Administration
Administered by a licensed physician following individual assessment
